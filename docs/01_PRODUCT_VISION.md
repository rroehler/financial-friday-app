# Financial Friday - Product Vision

## Mission

Help people make one better financial decision every week.

## Vision

Build a financial operating system that combines budgeting, debt management, cash flow planning, and financial coaching into one simple experience.

## Target Audience

### Initial
Ryan Roehler

### Future
Individuals who want a structured approach to improving their finances.

## Problem

Most budgeting apps show what happened.

Financial Friday focuses on what to do next.

## Success

A user can open the app once a week, follow the recommendations, and steadily improve their financial position.
